POWER FROM THE OTHER SIDE

Welcome to Power from the other side, your trusted partner for solar energy solutions in Abeokuta and across Nigeria. As a leading provider of solar installation services, solar equipment sales, and professional training, we are committed to bringing sustainable energy solutions to homes, businesses, and communities throughout the country.
Founded with the vision of reducing Nigeria’s dependence on unreliable power grids and advancing the adoption of clean, renewable energy, Power from the other side has become a key player in Nigeria's solar energy sector. Our team is passionate about delivering high-quality solar solutions that are tailored to meet the specific energy needs of our clients, ensuring a brighter, greener, and more sustainable future for all.
